Are public Wi-Fi networks secure?

In fact, it's usually quite safe to use a public Wi-Fi network for non-sensitive activities - especially if it's password-protected. However, there's always the risk that an attacker could use tactics like packet sniffing or man-in-the-middle attacks to steal your data, so it's best to avoid performing any sensitive activities like online banking while connected to a public Wi-Fi network.
